Experience

Chemistry YES competition - Young Entrepreneurs Scheme

In a team of four from University of York, I've taken part in the regional ChemistryYES competition held in Manchester (UK) sponsored by BBSRC, RSC and NERC. It involved:

  • 3-days training in business, IP, marketing and finance
  • team-working ahead of and during the event for building a business plan around a chemistry related commercial idea
  • pitching our business idea to fictionary investors (ChemistryYES judges)

Research Placement at CatSci Ltd

I've independently carried out a short project in a private business environment where I've received training on the site laboratory facilities, especially on automated technology for performing high-throughput reaction screening.

Summer project for Wella - Committed Beyond Beauty

I've participated in a P&G corporate sustainability project aiming to reduce the environmental impact of hair salons. My contribution involved reporting specific practices of concern by observing the daily routine within a number of salons and discussing potential issues and improvements together with salons' owners. The observations were presented at P&G Headquarters in Geneva and made into a promotional video and handbook.
