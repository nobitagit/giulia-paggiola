Education

Ph.D in Chemistry, University of York

Bio-based solvents constitute a valuable and sustainable alternative to conventional oi-derived solvent in a growing number of applications. In this project I investigate the feasibility of solvent substitution through performance comparison, industrial applicability, supply chain impact and industrial policy & strategy analysis.

This project is funded by IMI-Innovative Medicines Initiative and is part of the CHEM21 European consortium of research centres and pharmaceutical industries.

Fields of expertise

  • Experimental case-study: use of bio-based solvents in chemo-enzymatic synthesis of pharmaceuticals

  • Bio-based solvents standards

  • REACH and global chemicals regulations

  • Supply-chain mapping perception analysis

  • Stakeholder perception analysis
