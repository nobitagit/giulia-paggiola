Summary

Having performed research for pharmaceutical corporations I am interested in the drivers and the obstacles for the sustainable evolution of this sector. I have an aptitude to public relations and a drive to deal with change and challenging situations. My career objective is to work in policy-making and communication in relation to sustainability and corporate social responsibility.
